MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into by and between Stark Industries Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and Wayne Enterprises Ltd. ("Receiving Party").

This Agreement is effective as of 6/1/2023.

Each party may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to the other party for the purpose of evaluating a commercial collaboration.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for one (1) year from the Effective Date, unless earlier terminated by either party.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for two (2) years following the expiration or termination of this Agreement.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
