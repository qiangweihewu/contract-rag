NON-DISCLOSURE AND CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

This Agreement is entered into by and between Cyberdyne Systems Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and Soylent Foods LLC ("Receiving Party").

This Agreement is effective as of February 2, 2022.

The parties desire to exchange proprietary information concerning food technology research and automation for business evaluation purposes.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for four (4) years from the Effective Date.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for four (4) years after the termination or expiration of this Agreement.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Florida.
