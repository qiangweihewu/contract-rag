CONFIDENTIALITY AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into by and between Initech LLC ("Discloser") and Hooli Inc. ("Recipient").

This Agreement is effective as of September 9, 2025.

The parties desire to explore a potential technology partnership and wish to protect their respective proprietary information from unauthorized disclosure.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for five (5) years from the date first set forth above.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for seven (7) years from the date of disclosure or the termination of this Agreement, whichever is later.

At the request of the Discloser, the Recipient shall promptly return or destroy all copies of Confidential Information and certify such action in writing.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
