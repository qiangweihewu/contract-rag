MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into by and between Umbrella Pharma Corp. ("Disclosing Party") and Tyrell Biotech LLC ("Receiving Party").

The parties wish to share certain confidential information regarding pharmaceutical research and development for the purpose of exploring a joint venture.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for two (2) years from the date of last signature.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for four (4) years beyond the term of this Agreement.

The Receiving Party shall, upon written request, return or destroy any and all materials containing Confidential Information provided by the Disclosing Party.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
