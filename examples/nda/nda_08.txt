NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is entered into by and between Massive Dynamic LLC ("Disclosing Party") and Oscorp Industries Inc. ("Receiving Party").

This Agreement is effective as of July 4, 2025.

The purpose of this Agreement is to protect the confidential and proprietary information that may be shared between the parties in connection with evaluating a potential merger or acquisition.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for two (2) years from the Effective Date unless terminated earlier by mutual written consent.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for ten (10) years following any expiration or termination of this Agreement.

At the written request of the Disclosing Party, the Receiving Party shall return or destroy all Confidential Information and related notes and materials.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
