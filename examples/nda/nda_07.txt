MUTUAL NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement is made by and between Pied Piper Inc. ("Disclosing Party") and Aviato LLC ("Receiving Party").

This Agreement is effective as of 11/30/2024.

The Receiving Party agrees to hold all Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party in strict confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent.

This Agreement shall remain in full force and effect for three (3) years from the date of signing.

The obligations of confidentiality shall survive for five (5) years after the expiration or termination of this Agreement.

Upon the termination of this Agreement, the Receiving Party shall promptly return or destroy all tangible materials embodying Confidential Information.

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
